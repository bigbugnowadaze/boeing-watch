THE DEAD
PUBLIC RECORD · ALL CLAIMS CITED
01
JOHN BARNETT
09 MAR 2024 · AGE 62
Boeing quality manager, Charleston SC, thirty-two years of service.1 Found dead in his truck on the third day of his deposition in a whistleblower retaliation case against Boeing.2 Coroner ruled the death self-inflicted; subsequent police investigation attributed it to chronic stress from the case.3 In May 2025 Boeing settled the wrongful-death suit brought by his family. Terms were not disclosed.4 "I can't do this any longer." — note recovered from his vehicle.3
02
JOSHUA DEAN
01 MAY 2024 · AGE 45
Spirit AeroSystems quality auditor. Was the first to publicly raise concerns about misdrilled holes in 737 MAX aft pressure bulkheads, in 2022.5 Spirit fired him in April 2023; he filed a Department of Labor retaliation complaint in May.6 Two weeks after going into hospital with breathing problems, he was dead. MRSA infection, pneumonia, stroke.5 He was forty-five and reportedly in good health beforehand.7 "If you make too much trouble, you will get the Josh treatment." — Joshua Dean, NPR interview, Feb 2024.8
CR
ETHIOPIAN 302
10 MAR 2019 · 157 SOULS
737 MAX 8. Loss of control six minutes after takeoff from Bole International, Addis Ababa. MCAS activation traced to a single failed angle-of-attack sensor — the second such crash in five months.9 All 149 passengers and 8 crew killed.
CR
LION AIR 610
29 OCT 2018 · 189 SOULS
737 MAX 8. Loss of control thirteen minutes after takeoff from Soekarno–Hatta, Jakarta. MCAS activation traced to a single failed angle-of-attack sensor.10 All 181 passengers and 8 crew killed. Same root cause as Ethiopian 302 four months later.
